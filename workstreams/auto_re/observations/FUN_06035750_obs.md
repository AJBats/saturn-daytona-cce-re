## Overview

FUN_06035750 is called as sub-function #11 from the dispatcher (FUN_0604D380).
It manages a chain of 16-bit countdown timers at GBR+0x182 through +0x18C,
with threshold gates on +0x34 and +0x14 controlling activation. Also performs
sum-of-squares + sqrt computation and conditional writes to +0xC4, +0xF8, +0xD8.

## Timer Chain Structure

The function manages 6 consecutive 16-bit countdown timers at GBR+0x182:

| Offset | Timer # | Effect when counter reaches 0 |
|--------|---------|-------------------------------|
| +0x182 | 0 | Controls counter-based gating (sqrt block) |
| +0x184 | 1 | Set to 10 or 30 by steering/speed conditions |
| +0x186 | 2 | Clears +0x18E to 0 |
| +0x188 | 3 | Decays +0xF8 by multiplying with 0x000107AE |
| +0x18A | 4 | Sets +0xF8 to constant 0x09D58000 |
| +0x18C | 5 | Halves +0xF8, conditionally negates +0xC4 into +0xD8 |

All timers are -1 (inactive) at idle from dead stop. They activate when
speed and steering thresholds are met.

## Other Observations

- The magnitude computation uses sum-of-squares of three values
  (+0xF8, +0xEC, +0xC4) followed by a call to FUN_0604818C (integer sqrt).
  The result is clamped and written back to +0xC4.
- Timer #3 multiplies +0xF8 by 0x000107AE per frame when it fires.
  Timer #4 resets +0xF8 to the constant 0x09D58000.
- The threshold gate (+0x34 > 100 AND +0x14 > 0xE00) activates the
  counter increment path. Both conditions must be met simultaneously.
- Helper FUN_06035624 (from same TU) is NOT called from this function —
  it is called by FUN_060354A0 only.
